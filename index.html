Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my tickets?
Most tickets are delivered via mobile transfer or instant download. You can access them in your Ticketmaster account or via the venue's app.
Are tickets refundable?
Refunds depend on the event and the seller's policy. Many events offer FanProtect coverage. Check your order details for specific terms.
What is the official Ticketmaster resale?
Ticketmaster's official resale platform allows fans to buy and sell verified tickets at face value or market price, always with buyer protection.
Can I transfer my tickets to a friend?
Yes! Most tickets are transferable via your Ticketmaster account. Simply select the order and choose 'Transfer' to share with a friend.
